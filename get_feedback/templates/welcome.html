Up & running in minutes

Creating and managing surveys is super-simple. No signup or filling in of complicated forms required. Just create a new survey and follow the step-to-step guide to integrate it into your website.

Inline editing & results.

When your survey is running, you can edit elements directly on the website in which they're integrated. Likewise, you can also get a result summary for each element. Simple & direct.

Respects your visitors.

We do not record or log any personal information about your visitors and only store the feedback they provide you with.

Compatible with all browsers.

The survey elements are compatible with all modern browsers like Firefox, Chrome, Safari and even Internet Explorer (>=IE8).
